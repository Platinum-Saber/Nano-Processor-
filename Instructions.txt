Initially display will be zero. Next sum of 1 and 2 will be shown in the display.
3 is expected to be displayed. 
Output will be stored in register 7.

LED 0, LED 1, LED 2, LED 3 will shows the output came from R7.

Next the difference of 5 and 3 will be stored in register 7.
Output 2 is expected to be displayed.

Smaller, Equal, Greater will be shown by LED 08, LED 09 and LED 10.

Zero and Overflow will be shown by LED 14, LED 15 .

negative also represent in overflow LED 15.

Reset operation will be handle by the push button btnC.

Reset button will start the operation from 0.
